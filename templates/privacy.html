Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

At Spendly, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided during registration
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login timestamps, used solely for security and debugging purposes

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep your session secure
  • Store and display your expense records
  • Generate spending summaries and category breakdowns
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve reliability

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a secure database associated with your account. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or loss.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all your personal and expense data is permanently removed from our systems and cannot be recovered.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your expense records are private to your account and are never disclosed to other users or external organisations.

We may disclose data only if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request from a competent authority.

6. Third-Party Services

Spendly loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This request is made by your browser and is subject to Google's own privacy policy. No personal or financial data is transmitted to Google as part of this request.

We do not integrate with any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Cookies and Sessions

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains no personal information — it holds only a session identifier that expires when you log out or close your browser.

We do not use tracking cookies or any cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the details provided on our website. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
